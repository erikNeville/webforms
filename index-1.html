Reading and Language Arts

PopSmartkids's reading curriculum spans the entire range of early reading, from learning the names of each letter and the sounds they represent to being able to read books, starting with a few words on a page and gradually advancing to paragraphs.
The curriculum for first grade also includes writing and language skills, such as sentence structure and punctuation, parts of speech, and practice with writing genres.

Why PopSmartKids?

  • Curriculum includes hundreds of entertaining animated activities
  • Reading curriculum spans the entire range of early learning in kids
  • Teaches a broad range of subjects, including math and science
  • Keeps kids engaged with interactive activities in the form of fun games
  • Unique drawing and painting program for all ages
